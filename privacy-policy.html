Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2021

Who Are We

Faith Baptist Tabernacle operates https://www.myfbt.org

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of Personal Identifying Information (PII) we may receive from users of this website.

What Personal Data We Collect and Why We Collect It

The following sections detail what data we may collect as you use this website.

Cookies

A cookie is a small piece of encrypted text sent by a web server to your browser when you visit a website. A cookie file is stored in your browser and allows the website or a third-party to recognize you and to make the website more useful to you.

For instance, if you leave a comment on our site you may opt-in to saving your name, email address and website in cookies. These are for your convenience so that you do not have to fill in your details again when you leave another comment.

If you would like to delete cookies or instruct your browser to refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your browser.

Please note, however, that if you delete cookies or refuse to accept them, you may be unable to use all website features and you may be unable to store your preferences.

Embedded Content from Other Websites

This website may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracing your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics with anonymized IP addresses to obtain general information about our users geographic location, browser type, operating system, referral source, pages visited, and other analytics data.

We may review that data from time to time to understand how the website is used and to improve the website for users. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by

using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser add-on. You can access Googles privacy policy here: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/

The web server may log visitor information for the purpose of maintenance and security. This information may include the IP Address of the device you used to access this website.

We do not use web server log information to identify you nor combine log information with other analytics data to identify you. However, we may provide log information to the web host (Netlify) in case of a troubleshooting need.

Who We Share Your Data With

We will not provide your name, email address, or any other PII to any other person, other than if required to be disclosed by law or in connection with any actual or threatened legal proceeding.

How Long We Retain Your Data

We will keep your information for no longer than it is necessary for our purposes in accordance to our internal policies. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months. This is so we can identify trends in how users are accessing and how they are using this website.

Web server visitor log information is maintained for 30 days before being deleted.

What Rights You Have Over Your Data

You can also request that we erase any personal data we hold about you. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes. If you need any assistance with obtaining, changing, or removing your personal data, contact us at faithbaptist@sbcglobal.net

Additional Information

This website is directed to adults and not directed to children under the age of 13. We ask that minors not submit any PII. This website complies with the Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act and does not permit registration by, and will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from, anyone under 13 years of age.

We will take reasonable technical precautions to prevent the loss, misuse, or alteration of your PII. For instance, we use SSL to encrypt information that you send from your browser to the web server. If a data breach transpires, we will inform users of its occurrence within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach via email.

We do not track users after they visit this website. This website does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by posting an updated version on this webpage.

Will You Go to Heaven?

Salvation is a free gift of God. The death of Christ on the cross is the only sufficient payment for our sins. All have sinned, but all can be saved. This salvation is available for any who puts their trust in Christ as Savior and follows Him (Romans 3:23, 6:23; John 3:16).
