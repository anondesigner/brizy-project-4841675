Our Ministries

California Capitol Connection

California Capitol Connection is a ministry dedicated to empowering and urging God’s people to get responsibly involved in the governmental processes so liberties can be retained and maintained.

Global Missions

We currently support many missionaries across the world who seek to spread the Gospel.

Youth Ministry

We provide Bible-based, fun activities for young people ranging from Pre-K to 6th grade and 7th grade to 12th grade. Young people can have fun and grow in Christ.

Two Minute Warning Men's Meeting

Each year Faith Baptist Tabernacle organizes the Two Minute Warning Men’s Meeting. This is a time for men to take a timeout, evaluate where they are at spiritually, where they need to go, and to listen to the Lord about what to do next.

Spanish Ministry

Iglesia Bautista de Sacramento, a Spanish-speaking independent, fundamental Baptist church, holds church services at our location.

Tim Schmidt Ministries

Evangelist Tim Schmidt and his wife Megan travel throughout the country to encourage pastors and to win souls to Christ.

Will You Go to Heaven?

Salvation is a free gift of God. The death of Christ on the cross is the only sufficient payment for our sins. All have sinned, but all can be saved. This salvation is available for any who puts their trust in Christ as Savior and follows Him (Romans 3:23, 6:23; John 3:16).
