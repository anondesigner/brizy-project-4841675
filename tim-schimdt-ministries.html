Tim Schmidt Ministries

Preaching for Revival

Faith Baptist Tabernacle is blessed to serve as the home base of Tim Schmidt Ministries. Evangelist Tim Schmidt and his wife Megan travel throughout the country to encourage pastors and to win souls to Christ. The Schmidts are active in several ministries, including youth, music, and soulwinning.

To learn more about Tim Schmidt Ministries, please visit their website at the following link: https://evangelisttimschmidt.org/

Will You Go to Heaven?

Salvation is a free gift of God. The death of Christ on the cross is the only sufficient payment for our sins. All have sinned, but all can be saved. This salvation is available for any who puts their trust in Christ as Savior and follows Him (Romans 3:23, 6:23; John 3:16).
